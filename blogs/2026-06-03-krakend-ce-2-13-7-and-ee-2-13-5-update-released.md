KrakenD released patch updates (CE 2.13.7 and EE 2.13.5) that upgrade Go to version 1.25.11 to address three disclosed CVEs in the mime, net/textproto, and crypto/x509 packages. The security update is recommended for all KrakenD Community and Enterprise users.
