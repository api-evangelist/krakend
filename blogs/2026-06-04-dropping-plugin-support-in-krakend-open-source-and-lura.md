Beginning with KrakenD 3.0, Go plugin support is being removed from the Community Edition and the Lura framework, while remaining available in the Enterprise Edition. The post explains that Go plugins carry inherent complexity and support challenges across divergent open source build environments, and that source-level customization remains available as an alternative.
